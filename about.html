Nathan Irving is a real estate agent serving Marin and Sonoma County, California, with Irving Real Estate. His approach is built around three things: listening closely to what each client actually needs, doing the homework before it's asked for, and communicating clearly at every stage of a transaction.

Whether you're buying your first home, selling a longtime property, or relocating within the North Bay, the goal is the same — a process that feels organized, honest, and never rushed.

How I Work

What that looks like in practice

For Buyers

Touring homes with a clear sense of what fits your goals and budget, market context so you can write competitive offers with confidence, and steady guidance through inspections, financing, and closing.

For Sellers

A pricing strategy grounded in real comparable sales, prep and presentation advice that's actually worth the effort, and active management of showings, offers, and negotiations.

Where I Work

Most of my work happens across Marin and Sonoma County — from coastal towns like Sausalito and Mill Valley to inland communities like Santa Rosa and Healdsburg, and the smaller towns in between.
